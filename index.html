Rules – Short Version

Each player has 9 pits with 9 korgools (stones) in each, and a kazan (store). On your turn you choose one of your pits. If it has 1 stone, it moves to the next pit. If it has more than 1 stone, one stone stays and the rest are sown counter-clockwise into the next pits one by one.

If the last stone lands in an opponent’s pit and that pit now has an even number of stones, they are captured to your kazan. When a player reaches 82 or more stones, the game ends and the player with more stones wins. Basic tuz/sool rule is also supported.
